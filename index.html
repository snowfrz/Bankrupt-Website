BANKRUPT

Corporate Warfare

Play Now

War. Wealth. Victory.

Build an industrial empire and dominate the galaxy

Land on an alien planet, gather resources, and develop new weapons and technology to annihilate the competition. With over 18 quintillion unique planets to conquer, you will never run out of war to wage.

There are over 80 towers and units to choose from. Worlds are dynamic, so your actions can affect the terrain and atmosphere. There is also a supply-and-demand based market system to determine resource prices. Adapt as you play, and create your own unique strategies.

Like any good RTS, Bankrupt has a single-player campaign. Like any good RTS, it also supports creating and sharing custom levels and campaigns with the built-in Level Editor. Design your own planet, then set custom mission objectives. You get access to the exact same tools used to make the official Bankrupt campaign!

Enable Demigod Mode for a sandbox style experience. Build, destroy and terraform freely during a match to wreak havoc on players.

Bankrupt is now available the App Store for iPhone, iPad and Mac.
